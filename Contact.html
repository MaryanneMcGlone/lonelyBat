Kelly and Elizabeth McGlone are the creators of the beloved children's book, The Lonely Bat. This story is a halloween themed children's book about friendship. The Lonely Bat comes in two reading levels, preschool and elementary school. Gregory's search for friendship is a story enjoyed by children of all ages. We are selling copies year round. During the Fall, we read to children at local schools and festivals in Terre Haute, Indiana. We really enjoy reading to kids and introducing them to Gregory. If you would like us to visit your school or meet us at an event, please send us an email.
